Services

We believe that technology creates value, and it is a formula for success.

Consulting

We listen to your needs and provide suitable solutions according to it. Our team comes from different financial institutions and different departments, and has expertise in business, compliance, system integration and development. It is easy to understand your requirements and pain points, and will be able to provide prompt valuable advice.

We advocate for a solution with continuous development, allowing your product or service to be launched as soon as possible, and then continue to expand. It leads your company able to use resources more effectively according to market conditions.

Outsourcing

Technology is the key to success in financial industry nowadays. From user experience to internal operation, there must have a system to support. System requirements will only become more and more complex, and technology is developing rapidly. Mobile services, cloud services, big data, artificial intelligence, etc. all require developers to build according to the growth of the company. Therefore, we set up a development center in Indonesia, managed by Hong Kong to assure the quality. We help clients to develop, improve and connect systems, as well as provide system support, maintenance and knowledge transfer services.

Secondment

Sufficient and stable human resources are important to the project. Lack of qualified staff often leads to project delays. However, maintain a certain number of staff will increase the burden on the company, and recruitment is a long process too.

We offer talented professionals when you are experiencing staff shortages. They are mainly from financial industry and understand that the corporate structure and operation, hence to be able to integrate your company's culture and create value together.

About

Neomas is established by a group of professionals from the financial industry. We come from different positions. In addition to the IT department, but also the front, middle and back offices. Therefore, we are familiar with the operation of the industry and understand the exact requirements of users for the system.

Our team is familiar with the technology platforms on the market, and can quickly formulate the plans and put it online according to the needs of clients’ existing and new businesses. On the other hand, we use the cutting-edge technology to improve work efficiency through automation and reduce a lot of repetitive tasks and human errors.

Financial services are changing fast, and the compliance requirements are increasing day by day. There is a chronic shortage of suitable talents, and opportunities are often missed as a result. Therefore, please leave technology to us. You can focus more on product and business development, and even reduce the internal pressure.
